The Story of M&M

M & M Eyebrow + Lashes is where beauty meets precision. We specialize in eyebrows and lashes, offering expert threading, tinting and extensions designed to enhance your natural look. With every service, we focus on comfort, cleanliness, and results that last. At M & M, it’s not just about looking good – it’s about feeling confident in your own skin.
